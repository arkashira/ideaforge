# Customer Journey Map for Ideaforge

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event:** Indie hackers and creators experience frustration with the ideation process, leading them to seek solutions.
- **Friction Points:** Overwhelmed by the number of tools available; lack of awareness about AI-driven solutions specifically for ideation.
- **User Emotions:** Frustration, confusion, curiosity.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Engaging content marketing (blogs, webinars) that educates users on the ideation bottleneck and introduces Ideaforge as a solution.
- **Metric:** Number of website visits and engagement rates on educational content.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event:** Users actively search for tools to help with ideation after learning about Ideaforge.
- **Friction Points:** Uncertainty about the effectiveness of AI-generated ideas; skepticism about the value of the tool.
- **User Emotions:** Hopeful but cautious, intrigued, skeptical.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Free trials or demos showcasing the tool's capabilities; testimonials and case studies from successful indie hackers who used Ideaforge.
- **Metric:** Conversion rate from website visits to sign-ups for trials or demos.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event:** Users sign up for a trial or demo of Ideaforge.
- **Friction Points:** Initial learning curve; potential technical issues during setup or first use.
- **User Emotions:** Excitement mixed with anxiety; eagerness to see results.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Onboarding support (tutorials, live chat assistance); personalized idea generation based on user preferences.
- **Metric:** User engagement metrics during the trial period (e.g., number of ideas generated, time spent in the tool).

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event:** Users find value in the tool and decide to subscribe or purchase.
- **Friction Points:** Concerns about ongoing costs; fear of commitment to a subscription model.
- **User Emotions:** Relief, satisfaction, cautious optimism.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Clear communication of value proposition; flexible pricing plans; loyalty rewards for long-term users.
- **Metric:** Subscription conversion rate and churn rate post-adoption.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event:** Users successfully generate ideas and start implementing them, leading to potential upsell opportunities.
- **Friction Points:** Users may not be aware of additional features or services offered by Ideaforge.
- **User Emotions:** Empowerment, confidence, curiosity about advanced features.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Regular check-ins to suggest new features; educational content on maximizing the tool's potential; community engagement (forums, user groups).
- **Metric:** Upsell conversion rate and user engagement with advanced features.
