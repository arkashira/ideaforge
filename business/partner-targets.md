# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for ideaforge

### Target Integrations

| Partner/SaaS/API          | Free-Tier Limits                     | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job Solved)                                                                 | Affiliate/Revenue-Share |
|---------------------------|-------------------------------------|--------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Zapier**                | 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month             | M                  | Automate workflows by connecting ideaforge with other apps, reducing manual tasks.         | Yes                      |
| **Notion**                | 1,000 blocks per workspace          | S                  | Use Notion for organizing generated ideas, enhancing project management for creators.       | Yes                      |
| **Trello**                | 10 boards, unlimited cards          | S                  | Visualize and manage software ideas in a Kanban-style board, aiding in prioritization.     | Yes                      |
| **Slack**                 | 10 integrations, 5GB file storage   | S                  | Facilitate team communication and feedback on generated ideas directly within Slack.        | Yes                      |
| **Airtable**              | 1,200 records per base              | M                  | Organize and track ideas in a flexible database format, improving idea management.         | Yes                      |
| **Figma**                 | 3 projects, unlimited files          | M                  | Create visual mockups of software ideas, enhancing the ideation process with design tools. | Yes                      |
| **Google Sheets**         | 15GB storage                        | S                  | Easily manipulate and analyze generated ideas in a familiar spreadsheet format.             | No                       |
| **Product Hunt**          | N/A (community platform)            | L                  | Validate ideas by sharing them with a community of creators and receiving feedback.         | Yes                      |

### Rationale for Selection
1. **Zapier**: Enables seamless automation between ideaforge and other tools, allowing users to streamline their workflows. The affiliate program incentivizes partnerships.
2. **Notion**: Popular among indie hackers for project management; integrating with Notion allows users to organize ideas effectively. Revenue-share potential enhances collaboration.
3. **Trello**: Provides a visual management tool that aligns with the ideation process, making it easier for users to prioritize and track their ideas.
4. **Slack**: Enhances communication, allowing teams to discuss and refine ideas in real-time, which is crucial for collaborative ideation.
5. **Airtable**: Offers a powerful database solution for managing ideas, which is essential for users looking to keep track of multiple concepts.
6. **Figma**: Allows users to create visual representations of their ideas, which is important for those who prefer a design-oriented approach to ideation.
7. **Google Sheets**: While not directly tied to revenue-share, its widespread use makes it a valuable tool for data manipulation and analysis of generated ideas.
8. **Product Hunt**: Provides a platform for validation and feedback, essential for indie hackers looking to gauge interest in their ideas before development. 

### Conclusion
Integrating with these partners will enhance the ideaforge platform by addressing specific user jobs, improving workflow efficiency, and providing avenues for monetization through affiliate programs.
